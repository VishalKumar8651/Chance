Our Vision

A New Era of Luxury

"Fashion should not dictate who you are; it should amplify it."

It started with a vision — that every woman deserves to feel like the main character of her own story. Chance was born from a deep desire to create fashion that doesn't just cover the body, but celebrates the soul.

Our commitment to excellence ensures that every garment we create is a masterpiece, designed to empower and inspire.

Our Journey

2020

The Beginning

Chance was founded in a small studio in Mumbai with a single collection of 12 hand-sewn dresses that sold out in hours.

2022

Global Reach

We launched our international shipping, reaching women in over 15 countries and opening our first flagship store.

2024

Sustainable Shift

Achieved 100% carbon-neutral operations and transitioned to fully sustainable and ethical sourcing for all materials.

2026

The Future

Continuing to redefine luxury fashion with innovative designs, expanding our bridal collection, and celebrating women everywhere.
